Who is KFK?

KFK · 2019-06-22

On June 22, 2019, an anonymous user claiming to be from the year 2060 started a Q&A session in a Douban (Chinese social media) group. Using traditional Chinese characters and the ID "KFK", they claimed to have been born in Shanghai's Jing'an district in 2020 and traveled back to 2019 through a non-physical means.

In a short time, KFK answered 281 questions covering geopolitics, technology, climate disasters, social changes, philosophy, and spirituality. They provided many specific timelines and predictions, some of which have been confirmed by real-world events in the years since.

Regardless of KFK's true identity, these prophecies have become one of the most discussed "future person" events on the Chinese internet. This site compiles all 281 Q&A entries and evaluates the accuracy of predictions whose deadlines have passed.

Core Message
"If you can learn to shift your focus from material pursuits to spiritual faith in 2019, you won't suffer as much by 2048-2060."
