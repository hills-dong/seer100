Who is Peter Deunov?

Peter Deunov · 1900-1944

Peter Deunov (July 11, 1864 – December 27, 1944), spiritual name Beinsa Douno, was a Bulgarian spiritual teacher, philosopher, and musician who founded the Universal White Brotherhood. He graduated from Boston University School of Theology and developed a unique spiritual teaching system blending Christian mysticism with Eastern philosophy.

Throughout his life, Deunov delivered over seven thousand lectures, leaving extensive teachings on humanity's spiritual evolution. His most widely circulated work is his 1944 deathbed prophecy, describing Earth's imminent great transformation — the coming of the Sixth Race, the establishment of a civilization of love, and the collective elevation of human consciousness.

Albert Einstein reportedly said of Deunov: 'The whole world bows to me, and I bow to the Master Peter Deunov.' This site compiles Deunov's core prophecies and teachings, covering the Sixth Race, Earth's transformation, WWII predictions, and the Slavic mission, with verification analysis for expired predictions.

Core Message
