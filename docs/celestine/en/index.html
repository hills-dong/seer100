What is The Celestine Prophecy?

James Redfield · 1993

The Celestine Prophecy is a spiritual adventure novel published in 1993 by American author James Redfield. The story follows a protagonist's journey through the Peruvian rainforest in search of an ancient manuscript containing Nine Insights about humanity's spiritual evolution.

Originally self-published, the book quickly became a global phenomenon, spending over three years on the New York Times bestseller list and selling over 23 million copies worldwide in 34 languages. It is considered a landmark work of the 1990s New Age movement, profoundly shaping public understanding of synchronicity, energy awareness, and spiritual growth.

Redfield later published sequels The Tenth Insight (1996) and The Secret of Shambhala (1999), expanding the insights from nine to twelve. This site compiles the core prophecies from the series and evaluates their correspondence with real-world trends.

Core Message
"When enough individuals reach a critical mass of awakening, this consciousness will spread unstoppably through the entire culture, and we will build an entirely new civilization based on spiritual truth."
