Who is KFK 2075?

KFK2075 · 2018-2020

KFK 2075, known as YJ (@YJ943636) on Twitter, registered on January 3, 2018, claiming to be a time traveler from 2075. Posting in Japanese, YJ answered questions about the future covering technology, warfare, geographical changes, and social systems.

In KFK's (the time traveler from 2060) Douban Q&A, KFK mentioned that there were 'legal time travelers' from both 2075 and 2062, stating that 'most of their answers are truthful, though some are well-intentioned white lies.' This brought YJ's predictions significant attention in the Chinese-speaking internet.

YJ posted intermittently from 2018 to 2020, claiming to have foreseen satellite crashes, World War III, Japan's capital relocation, an AI-dominated society, and more. In December 2018, YJ suddenly stopped posting and left behind a mysterious encrypted message, briefly returning in early 2020 before disappearing again. This site compiles the major predictions with verification assessments.
