Who is Kim Clement?

Kim Clement · 2006-2016

Kim Clement (September 30, 1956 – November 23, 2016), born in Cape Town, South Africa, was a charismatic Christian prophet, musician, and worship leader. After struggling with drug addiction in his youth, he experienced a spiritual transformation and claimed to receive the gift of prophecy.

Clement was known for his distinctive 'singing prophecy' style — he often delivered prophetic messages while improvising at the piano. His TV show 'Prophetic Alert' aired globally and attracted a large following. His prophetic style was passionate, often declaring prophecies publicly at large gatherings.

Clement's most famous prediction was that Donald Trump would become president of the United States — a prophecy made years before Trump announced his candidacy. He also prophesied about scientific breakthroughs, economic shifts, and Middle East events. Clement passed away in November 2016, shortly after Trump's election, at age 60.

Core Message
"Trump shall become a president. God says, I will put him in that place, and he shall be an unusual president for two terms."
